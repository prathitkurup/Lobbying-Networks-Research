    overlap is substantially higher — motivating community analysis.

-- Interpretation --
  Co-lobbying is 24x above random chance at the median pair,
  despite the global sparsity. This is the core justification for:
  (1) treating co-lobbying edges as a meaningful coordination signal,
  (2) using community detection to find coordinated lobbying coalitions,
  (3) studying cross-industry connectors (firms bridging coalitions).
