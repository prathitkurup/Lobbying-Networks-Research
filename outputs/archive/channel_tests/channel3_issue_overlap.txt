  SUPPORT for correlated-response hypothesis: directed pairs have

  Issue similarity is a significant predictor of RBO edge weight
  (ρ = 0.137), meaning firms in the same policy domain not only
  form directed pairs — they are more strongly similar in their
  ranked bill portfolios. This is the strongest evidence for the
  correlated-response channel.
